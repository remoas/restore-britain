Our Principles

What We Stand For

Six pillars. No compromise. Every policy voted on by you.

Secure Borders

End mass immigration. Deport all illegal migrants. Britain's borders, Britain's rules.

Small Government

Slash the state. Cut taxes. Empower individuals and enterprise, not bureaucrats.

Direct Democracy

Every policy voted on by members. Your voice matters — not Westminster's.

British Culture

Proud of our history, our language, our heritage. No apologies. No surrender.

Economic Freedom

Sound money. End reckless spending. Ban quantitative easing. Reward hard work.

Accountability

Halve the size of Parliament. End the Westminster gravy train. The Great Repeal Act 2029.
